My Hobbies

Art

Ever since I could hold a crayon, I have been drawing. I littered the refridgerators of both parents and grandparents with my creations. I started doing some art for a profession in my early teens and into my twenties. I started using alchohol markers and never went back to other media. I fell in love with them. This is my first drawing with the Copic markers.

Cooking

In my youth I was a very picky eater. One day in exasperation at the level of my pickiness, my mother suggested I make my own food. I don't think she expected me to take her words literally, but I have been cooking and baking ever since. I love watching shows, dining in restaurants, and reading books for inspiration. I thouroughly enjoy making food for others as well as myself. Some of my favorite meals to prepare are:

  • Breakfast
  • Polenta, Mushrooms, Poached Eggs, and Marinara
  • Chicken Tikka Masala, Forbidden Rice, and Garlic Cheese Naan

Gardening

My most recent hobbies stems (no pun intended) from my love of food. I wanted to try growing my own veggies and herbs. I started with some transplants from the farmers' market. I babied them and hovered over them with the enthusiasm and fear of a helicopter parent. They grew a little bit, but as soon as I stepped away, they blossomed and pretty soon I could not get them to stop! The second year, I grew them all from seed. What started like this:
